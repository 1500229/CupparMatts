Cupar Mutts

Cupar Mutts is a Doggy Day care business based in Cupar.

The company was established in 2012, and has looked after dogs from the North East of Fife ever since. Our Doggy Day Care is excellent for socilaising your dog. many dog owners forget that it is not natural for their dogs to be in contact with a human all day, every day. Cupar Mutts

  • Helps your dog learn how to behave around other dogs
  • Tires your dog out for when you collect at the end of the day
  • Provides basic training for your dog
  • Walks your dog in a large group along the Fife coast when weather permits
